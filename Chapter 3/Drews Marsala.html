Chicken Marsala

By: Drew Ortiz

I love this recipe for Chicken Marsala. Especially since I love mushrooms!!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Shataki Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • Moar Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Marsala Wine
  • Thyme
  • Heavy Cream

Recipe

  1. Make chiken 1/4 to 1/2 inches thick. To do this you might have to butterfly the chicken or pound it out.
  2. Cut shitake mushrooms in strips.
  3. Smash and peel three cloves of garlic, no need to chop them.
  4. Put 2-4 tbsps of butter in a pan. Allow butter to melt.
  5. Once the butter starts to bubble add 3-5 fresh sprigs of thyme. Cook for about a minute.
  6. Add the mushrooms and garlic to the pan. Add salt and pepper to your liking. Cook until the mushrooms are nice and sauteed.
  7. Coat chicken breasts in flour.
  8. In another pan, add some olive oil over medium/high heat. Cook chicken for 3-5 minutes.
  9. Just before the chicken starts to brown add 1 cup of Marsala Wine.
  10. Reduce heat to medium. Cook until it starts to reduce, about 5 minutes.
  11. Add the mushrooms, garlic and thyme to the pan with the chicken.
  12. Add 1/4 cup of heavy cream. Mix it all up and let it cook until it thickens. I usually take a spoon and dip it in the sauce, then when it sticks to the back of the spoon it is my liking of thickness.
  13. Plate the chicken and top with the mushrooms.
    • Disguard the thyme and garlic.

Chicken
Meat from a bird. Breasts are best for this recipe.
Thyme
A herb used to add taste.
Can be fresh or dried. I suggest using fresh.
